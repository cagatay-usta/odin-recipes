S'jirra's famous potato bread

Ingredients:

  • 1 large potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 2 packets dry yeast
  • 6 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp flour/li>

Steps:

  1. Boil the potato cubes for about 15 minutes, or until soft. Do not drain, but set aside ½ cup of the cooking liquid. Mash together the potato and remaining liquid.
  2. Preheat oven to 190C/375F. Before baking, brush tops with a bit of cold water and dust with additional flour for a crisp crust.
  3. Bake for 40 minutes, or until the crust is dark brown. Remove from pans and cool on wire rack.
